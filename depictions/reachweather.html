ReachWeather

Description

  • ReachWeather puts the current weather conditions in your Reachability view (on supported devices).

  • Weather data courtesy of the OpenWeatherMap API.

  • NOTE: This is an initial beta release. I don't guarantee that everything will work right.

Changelog for version 0.1-3

  • • Fixed bug where a city with spaces in the name would not generate weather data.

  • • Added repo depiction.

How to use ReachWeather

  • • Navigate to Settings > ReachWeather

  • • Type in your city name into the space provided and hit "Return".

  • • Hit the "Back" button.

  • • You can now activate Reachability (by double-tapping TouchID) and view your current weather conditions while in any app.

Compatibility

  • ReachWeather works with iOS 8.0+ and iDevices that support Reachability (iPhone 6/6+).
