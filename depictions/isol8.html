Isol8

Description

  • Isol8 supercharges the native iOS 8 message muting functionality, blocking ALL notifications from muted conversations, including banner, notification center, and lock screen alerts.

Changelog for version 1.1-2

  • • Added support for muting individual conversations.

  • • Conversations muted prior to installing Isol8 will now be muted upon installation. (NOTE: you must quit and restart the Messages app in order for this to occur.)

How to use Isol8

  • • Open Settings > Isol8 and enable it if it isn't already.

  • • Go into a conversation in Messages.

  • • Tap "Details" in the top right.

  • • Scroll down until you see the "Do Not Disturb" option; turn this on.

  • • Isol8 will (by default) block all banners, notification center alerts, and lock screen alerts for this conversation.

Compatibility

  • Isol8 works with iOS 8.0+ and all iDevice models.
