Isol8

Description

  • Isol8 supercharges the iOS 8 group message muting functionality, blocking all forms of notifications from the group messages you specify, including blocking banners, notification center notifications, and lock screen notifications.

Changelog for version 0.0.3

  • Big revert; tweak is back to working state again (hopefully)!

How to use Isol8

  • Open Settings > Isol8 and enable it if it isn't already.

  • Go into a group message in Messages.

  • Tap "Details" in the top right.

  • Scroll down until you see the "Do Not Disturb" option; turn this on.

  • Isol8 will automatically block banners, notification center alerts, and lock screen alerts for this group message.

Compatibility

  • Isol8 works with iOS 8.0+ and all iDevice models.
